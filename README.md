# TouchGrass

Block distracting apps with a tap. Unblock with another tap.

TouchGrass uses an NFC tag to lock and unlock your apps.

## How It Works

1. Pick the apps you want to block
2. Tap your phone on the NFC tag to lock them
3. When you're ready, tap the tag again to unlock

You get 3 emergency unlocks in case you don't have your tag nearby.

## What You Need

- iPhone XS or newer (iOS 16+)
- An NFC tag (NTAG215 or NTAG216 — a few dollars online)

## Getting Started

1. Download TouchGrass (App Store link coming soon)
2. Open the app and grant Screen Time permission
3. Choose which apps to block
4. Tap the sparkle icon to program your NFC tag
5. Hold a blank NFC tag near the top of your iPhone
6. Done — you're all set
